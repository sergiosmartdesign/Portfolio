Senior Art Director & Strategic Designer. With over a decade of experience (since 2012), I have developed a multidisciplinary perspective that seamlessly merges Art Direction with Brand Strategy. Based in Bogotá, Colombia, I approach projects with a global vision, focusing on the rigorous analysis of a client's Brand DNA to implement it across various media: from web architecture and user experience design, to photography, illustration, and comprehensive branding.

As a UX/UI designer and visual strategist, I maintain a sharp eye for detail and a deep interest in functionality that effectively communicates meaning. My goal is to transform a brand's vision into engaging digital and tangible experiences that are clear, authentic, and create a memorable impact. I currently work as an independent freelance professional, helping my clients tell their authentic stories through design.

My work is an extension of my lifestyle: I am constantly observing how people interact with space, technology, and objects. I look for aesthetics everywhere, whether in the forms of nature, architecture, urban colors, or everyday details. For me, every project is more than a task; it is a story that I help the brand tell with authenticity and emotion. I strongly believe that good design is not just about colors and fonts, but about the feelings and connections it manages to evoke.

Brand DNA as Your Compass. My process begins with listening. It's not just about delivering a design, but about co-creating a visual language that is a faithful extension of your identity. I view your Brand DNA not as a static manual, but as the emotional and strategic compass that must guide every pixel, every stroke, and every interaction. My expertise in Strategic Art Direction is dedicated to unraveling that essence and translating it coherently into any format you need—whether it’s relaunching your web platform, defining your photographic style, or conceptualizing your entire branding. Let's work together to ensure your design doesn't just look good, but feels authentic and delivers a tangible impact on your audience.
