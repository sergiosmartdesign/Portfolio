Certificate Gallery Demo

Controls:

  • Drag - Drag cards left/right to navigate
  • Click - Click any card to center it
  • Prev/Next - Use buttons to navigate
  • Arrow Keys - ← → to navigate
  • ESC - Close the gallery
  • × Button - Close the gallery

JavaScript API:

  • window.certGallery.open() - Open gallery
  • window.certGallery.close() - Close gallery
  • window.certGallery.next() - Next card
  • window.certGallery.prev() - Previous card
  • window.certGallery.goTo(index) - Go to card
